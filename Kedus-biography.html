Biography

Kedus Kinfe was the one of the first Dutch-born Ethiopian football players to ever play in the FXA soccer league. He has stayed loyal to his childhood favorite club, Gorkha FC, also becoming the league's youngest MVP. Kedus joined the club as part of its youth academy.

During the off-season, Kedus spent his time studying for his bio-chemical-nuclear-physical engineering degree at the pretigious George Mason University.

He has since been actively supporting the All In Challenge to help people in need. He also likes to surprise kids with the Make a Wish foundation by facetiming them. "Watching those kids smile means the world to me. I would trade any of my FXA MVP trophies to see just one kid smile," Kedus told the foundation in 2018.

Now Kedus also serves as Chairman on the board of the Habesha Youth Foundation.
